Good Morning Everyone,



At this moment,

I woke from a dream

and can no longer see

heaven.

The ladder has lifted.

Or maybe was never there.

(We can never be sure.)

The voice is silent:

I am left to sit alone

In its echo.

This is the ground

of being itself

and I sit upon it

with no fear.



Be well.
