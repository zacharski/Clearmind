Creation



 On the evening

Of the first day

I created myself.


"Self," I said, "Go

Forthwith

Take control

Of yourself


Name yourself

And all living

Things:


Be Kind\

Be Gentle

And Love

Your world."


All these things

I said to myself

And then went

On my way.


Daiho
